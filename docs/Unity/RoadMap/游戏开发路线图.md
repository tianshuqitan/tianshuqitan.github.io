## 物理(Physics)

* 动力学(Dynamics)
  * 质心(Center of Mass)
  * 转动惯量(Moment of Inertia)
  * 加速度(Acceleration)
  * 关节(Joints)
  * 恢复系数(Restitution)
  * 力(Force)
  * 角速度(Angular Velocity)
  * 浮力(Buoyancy)
    * [Water interaction model for boats in video games](https://www.gamedeveloper.com/programming/water-interaction-model-for-boats-in-video-games)
    * [Water interaction model for boats in video games: Part 2](https://www.gamedeveloper.com/programming/water-interaction-model-for-boats-in-video-games-part-2)

* 碰撞检测(Collision Detection)
  * 连续碰撞检测(CCD)
  * 粗略阶段(Broad Phase)
    * 包围体(Bounding Volume)
      * 轴对齐包围盒(Axis-Aligned Bounding Box(AABB))

      * 排序与剪裁(Sort and Sweep)

      * 动态包围体树(Dynamic Bounding Volume Tree(DBVT))
        * [Dynamic Bounding Volume Hierarchies](https://sopiro.github.io/DynamicBVH/)
  * 精确阶段(Narrow Phase)
    * 相交(Intersection)
      * SAT 算法(Separating Axis Theorem)
        * [SAT(Separating Axis Theorem)](https://dyn4j.org/2010/01/sat/)
      * EPA 算法
        * [EPA(Expanding Polytope Algorithm)](https://dyn4j.org/2010/05/epa-expanding-polytope-algorithm/)
      * GJK 算法(Gilbert–Johnson–Keerthi)
    * 凸性(Convexity)
      * 凸体(Convex)
        * 凸包(Convex Hull)
      * 凹(Concave)
        * 凸分解(Convex Decomposition)
